About me

It's pretty safe to say that lockdown 2020 was rough for everyone but for anyone fresh out of high school, with no idea what they wanted to do, "the pressure was getting worser". I, at that point, had no interest in computers or anything related to tech but since I had nothing better to do, I gave it a shot.

To my massive surprise, I turned out to be pretty decent. I landed a few freelancing jobs in web development along the way then ended up with the pleasure of working at a brand consultancy based in the United States.

I've come a long way since my origin story, and I've come to really enjoy building interactive websites with an emphasis on animations and bringing every page to life. I always experiment and do more research to find the best ways to give sites an almost "video-game-like" feel to them as much as I can.

When I'm not slamming my head against the desk by my computer trying to rid myself of every error that appears in the web console, I quite enjoy an extremely cheeky game of soccer and other sports as well as making the most of my time spent with close friends. I am also currently studying software engineering at Hyperion.
